Algo Alpha Bot — How It Works

A daily automated trading pipeline using momentum & mean reversion strategies

Daily Pipeline
🌐 1. Fetch Market Data universe.ts
Pulls 60 daily candles (OHLCV) for all 22 symbols — stocks via Finnhub, crypto via CoinGecko.
📐 2. Calculate Indicators indicators.ts
Computes EMA (20), RSI (14), ATR (14), and Bollinger Bands (20, 2σ) for each symbol using the candle history.
🔍 3. Detect Market Regime regime-detector.ts
Classifies each asset as TRENDING or RANGING by checking EMA slope (≥ 0.5% per period) and whether ATR is expanding.
⚡ 4. Generate Signal signal-engine.ts
Picks the right strategy for each regime (see below), then produces a BUY / SELL / HOLD signal with a conviction score (0–1).
📏 5. Size the Position position-sizer.ts
Uses Half-Kelly Criterion scaled by conviction score. Hard cap of 5% of portfolio per trade. Whole shares for stocks, 6 decimals for crypto.
✅ 6. Execute Trade trade-executor.ts
Calls the trading engine directly (no HTTP). Skips execution if signal is HOLD or position size is zero. Logs outcome.
Strategy Selection (Step 4)
Trending Market
Momentum Strategy
BUY when price crosses above 20 EMA and RSI is 50–70
SELL when price crosses below 20 EMA or RSI > 80

Conviction = EMA slope + RSI strength + volume surge
Ranging Market
Mean Reversion Strategy
BUY when price touches lower Bollinger Band and RSI < 35
SELL when price reaches middle band or RSI > 65

Conviction = distance to band + RSI oversold depth
